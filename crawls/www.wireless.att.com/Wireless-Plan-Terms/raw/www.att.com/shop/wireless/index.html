    The Best Network

    AT&T has 4G coverage nationwide, bringing faster speeds to more than 200 million Americans. With the introduction of 4G LTE, AT&T is also the only carrier that offers two layers of network technology that deliver 4G speeds: LTE and HSPA+.

    Why does that matter? You'll enjoy fast 4G speeds on both the HSPA+ network and on LTE where available. That means a smoother, more consistent 4G experience overall.

    Levels of service include:

    • LTE – Long Term Evolution. Speeds up to 10x faster than 3G. That's what we have in mind as we continue to boost speed and performance in more and more places. Learn more at att.com/network.
    • HSPA+ – Technology that enables speeds up to 4x faster than AT&T's already fast mobile broadband network.
    • HSPA – The 3G technology that powers our fast mobile broadband network today.

     

    • UMTS – UMTS (Universal Mobile Telephone Service) is the next level of service. This 3G technology enables vertical–specific devices, video and music on-demand, digital TV and radio, and services such as home or business security video monitoring.
    • EDGE – Further enhancements in data capability over the GSM network are provided with EDGE service (Enhanced Data rates for GSM Evolution). EDGE is especially important to business users as it permits mobile access to corporate applications at speeds far in excess of dialup.
    • GPRS – GPRS (General Packet Radio Service) is the first level of data service on GSM. GPRS allows nationwide text, email service, and basic Internet browsing.
